Real World: Unintended Consequences
____________________________________________________________

Unintended Consequences:

A media users' bond with media personas can lead to higher self-confidence, a stronger perception of problem focused coping strategies, and a stronger sense of belonging. A consuming parasocial relationship can lead to neglecting real world relationships like friendships. In worst cases, can impair emotional maturity and fulfillment.



Swatting:

Stream viewers/ content consumers occasionally seek out key information such as a house address, venue address, airport gate, etc. Then they call the logical police/ authorities and report a bomb threat or other that induces a SWAT team mobilization In the worse cases, streamers and house tenets can be temporarily arrested, held at gunpoint, etc. Streamers are occasionally forced to move, or establish deep connections with the local PD to neutralize incoming threats.



Stalking:

Streamers, celebtrities, and public figures often get their addresses leaked, which can lead to stalking, unwanted visitors, etc
