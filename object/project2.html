Motor Buffet

Summary

This project was all about learning how to wire the main motor types (servo, dc, stepper), and control them using arduino and code. Very Fun! Our group decided that a new and unique implementation of the various motor types would be to have a swordfight! Two of my groupmates wired joysticks to DC motors and attached precariously 3D printed swords to the top. In the interest of variety, I used the potentiometer knob assembly included in the arduino kit to wire a servo motor. On top, I attached a small cutout of legendary swordswoman Beatrix "The Bride" Kiddo.
